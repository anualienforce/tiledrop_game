# Privacy Policy for TileDrop

**Last Updated: October 2, 2025**

## Introduction

TP GAMES ("we," "our," or "us") operates the TileDrop mobile game application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our game.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Game Progress Data**: Your scores, level progress, power-ups, and achievements are stored locally on your device
- **Settings Preferences**: Audio settings, accessibility preferences (color-blind mode, high contrast, reduced motion)
- **Anonymous Usage Data**: Game sessions, feature usage, and app crashes (if analytics is enabled)

### Information We DO NOT Collect
- We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
- We do not track your location
- We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
- We do not share data with third parties

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Save your game progress locally on your device
- Remember your settings and preferences
- Improve game performance and fix bugs
- Enhance your gaming experience

## Data Storage

- All game data is stored **locally on your device** using browser localStorage
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- Your data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or third parties

## Children's Privacy

TileDrop is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally, no personal information is transmitted or stored on our servers.

## Third-Party Services

We do not currently use third-party analytics or advertising services. If we add such services in the future, we will update this policy and notify users.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your game data at any time by:
1. Opening Settings in the game
2. Using the "Reset All Data" option
3. Or uninstalling the app from your device

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
